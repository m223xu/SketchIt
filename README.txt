Additional feature:
Scale shapes:
For a line, press one of two end points of the line and drag it, the unpressed point is fixed.
For a rectangle, press the bottom right corner to resize it.
For an ellipse, press the rightmost point and drag to change its horizontal radius,
or press and drag the bottom point to change its vertical radius.
